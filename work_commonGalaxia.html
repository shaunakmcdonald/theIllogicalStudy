Common Galaxia



‘Common Galaxia’ is a cafe project from husband and wife duo Luke Mutton and Kylie Mackinlay.

Illogical Study was commissioned to design and make draftsman-style stools in american oak and stainless steel.

These stools can also be purchased from our online store.

Materials: Solid American Oak & 304 Stainless Steel
