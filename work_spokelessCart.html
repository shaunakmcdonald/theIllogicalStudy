Spokeless Cart



The spokeless cart was designed and made for Patricia Coffee Brewers, Melbourne CDB.

The spoke-less design enables un-obscured display of the cart contents and also eliminates any danger posed to those accessing the contents, should the cart get knocked.

The carts generous wheel size allows movement over uneven terrain.

Simple timber components can be arranged in a number of configurations on the stainless steel frame.

Materials: American Oak, Stainless Steel and Brass

Umbrella by Basil Bangs
Fitout by Foolscap Studios
