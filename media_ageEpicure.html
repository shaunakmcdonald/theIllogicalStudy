The AGE Epicure mention for Illogical Study’s ‘Common Galaxia’ stools



Illogical Study received mention in the Age Epicure Section (24 April 2012) for stools designed and produced for cafe Common Galaxia.

Common Galaxia, serves outstanding specialty coffee and food in Seddon, from husband and wife duo Luke Mutton and Kylie Mackinlay, also of Dead Man Espresso fame.
