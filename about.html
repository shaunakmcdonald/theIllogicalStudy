Illogical Study is about producing furniture and objects sustainably.

We source enduring materials, so that our products have long lives; we reuse, recycle or otherwise source from local Australian and environmentally certified avenues.

It is this and an attention to detail that sets apart Illogical study products.

Design is about problem solving and is a means to improve the quality and functionality of our lives. Through an abstract interpretation of the world, The Illogical Study creates pure solutions that result in intelligent, well-crafted products.

Illogical Study makes and designs all their products in Australia. Their primary workspace is at NC Makers Space is North Coburg.
